What We Monitor

Six Pillars of Mental Wellbeing

Comprehensive daily tracking that understands your mind and body — and alerts loved ones when you need support.

🎭

Mood & Emotions

Daily emotion check-ins with AI pattern detection to identify depression, anxiety, or mood disorders early.

🌙

Sleep Patterns

Monitor sleep quality, duration and consistency — poor sleep is one of the earliest signs of mental health changes.

🏃

Physical Activity

Track movement and exercise — activity levels directly correlate with mental health and motivation.

💬

Social Interaction

Detect withdrawal, isolation or conflict patterns in your social interactions.

🧠

Psychological State

Advanced psychological MCQ assessments based on PHQ-9, GAD-7 and similar validated instruments.

🔔

Family Alerts

Automatically notifies your trusted contacts — family or counselors — when risk indicators are detected.
